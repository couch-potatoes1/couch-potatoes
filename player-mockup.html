Also fixing the broken video

The TVs placeholder you saw is YouTube's "video unavailable" graphic. Since the trailer worked on your husband's phone but not your desktop, the most likely cause is third-party-cookie blocking on Chrome desktop — YouTube's standard youtube.com/embed URL relies on cookies that desktop tracking protection blocks.

Switching the embed to youtube-nocookie.com (YouTube's own privacy-enhanced embed mode) sidesteps that. I also dropped autoplay=1, since desktop browsers block autoplay without a prior user gesture and that can produce the same broken state.
