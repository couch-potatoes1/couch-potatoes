Quick reality check

Both players are identical in size — full content width, 16:9 aspect ratio. Option A is the same size as Option B. The "no tiny video" worry isn't actually at play here; that would only be true if the trailer literally replaced the 90×135 poster thumbnail, which is not what either option does.

The real tradeoff is: A keeps the title/rating/genres visible during playback (so you can see what you're watching as you watch), while B gives you a dedicated "stop the trailer" button at the cost of hiding that metadata.
