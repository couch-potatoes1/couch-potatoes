Profile review screen — proposed layout

Three intents, no destructive escape hatch. Two states depending on whether the user named a partner.

After · partner named

Three intents in priority order: invite, fill in for them, or go solo. Solo is visible but quiet — it's a real option, not a panic button.

After · no partner named

No preferences set

When no partner name was entered, "fill it out for them" makes no sense — there's no them. Solo gets promoted to a real secondary CTA.

What changed

  • · "Set up Partner 2's profile" → "I'll fill it out for them" (intent, not data model)
  • · "Invite your partner" → "Invite [name]" when a partner name exists
  • · "Start over" removed from this screen entirely
  • · Solo path promoted from hidden tertiary to a real visible option
  • · On the no-partner variant, the "fill in for them" button is hidden so the stack doesn't feel redundant
